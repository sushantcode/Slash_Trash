    Slash Trash is a green company that is dedicated to reduces the trash produced by the food places on a daily basis. It promotes the uses of reusable items by providing incentives to customers. We keep track of the reusuable items used, points gained by the customers, and also the waste points saved by the resturants.

    How to use?

    • In Adding New Data Section, find the table you want to add record to and click "Insert 'Table_Name'" and fill out the fields and click Add.
    • In Update Data Section, find the table you want to update and click "Update 'Table_Name'" and fill out the fields and click Update.
    • To Display data, select table from dropdown, and hit display.
    • To delete record, select table from dropdown, provide Id of the entity, and clisk delete button.
    • To find how much points a particluar customer gained from the given range of dates, just provide the customer ID, the beinging dtae and the end date. Then click View.
    • Last but not the least, to add bonus point to customers who visited a particular Establishment on a particluar date, provide Establishment ID the customer visited, the date and the bonus points to be added. Finally click ADD to update the original table.
